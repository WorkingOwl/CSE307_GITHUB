LASTEST NEWS

Premium Bandai posted the official images and the promo video of the Complete Selection Modification Dark Kivat Belt from the Kamen Rider Kiva series. The item comes with Kivat-Bat II, a buckle set, and the following Fuestles, namely: Wake Up Fuestle (Dark Kiva), Garulu Seal Fuestle, Basshaa Seal Fuestle, Dogga Seal Fuestle, Doran Fuestle (Dark Kiva), and Buroon Fuestle (Dark Kiva).

This item is used by three different users, namely: King, Otoya Kurenai, and Taiga Nobori to transform into Kamen Rider Dark Kiva. The included Kivat-Bat II has been redesigned compared to the DX version when it comes to modeling and painting to make it look like the one seen in the series. Also included in this item is are sound effect and BGM buttons on both ears, and a dialogue button and a microphone for voice recognition on the back. The built-in voice recognition gimmick recognizes specific words spoken to Kivat-Bat II, which is on standby for voice recognition, and activates a response corresponding to that word. In addition, even if you'll say a different word, he can respond with a different line, giving you this feeling that you are having a conversation with Kivat-Bat II. Just like the CSM Kivat-Bat III, the item also has an Accelerometer, in which moving Kivat-Bat II around will activate distinct flight sounds by moving him at different speeds. Tomokazu Sugita has recorded multiple lines as Kivat-Bat II, so that you can enjoy the item not only as a transformation device, but as a character.
